Welcome to the Land of Ooo!

The Land of Ooo is home to characters Finn and Jake, and both their friends and foes on the show ADVENTURE TIME. The land is divided into different kingdoms. The most prominent being the Ice Kingdom, the Candy Kingdom, the Earldom of Lemongrab, Wildberry Kingdom, the Fire Kingdom and the Cloud Kingdom. Ooo is a post-apocalyptic version of Earth that resulted from a global disaster called the Mushroom War. Inhabitants consist of diverse, mutated, enhanced, elemental, and sometimes robotic, magical-beings, making the Land of Ooo magical and an adventure itself.
