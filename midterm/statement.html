Design Choices

I chose the type faces "Luckiest Guy" and "Fredoka One" because I thought the complimented eachother well. I was going for a cartoony and playful aesthetic and thought those were the best fit because they were fun. I wanted the headers to be Luckiest Guy because they stood out more and were bold. They are fun, yet are still legible for the user.

When I began the home page, I first looked for an Adventure Time background image and came across the one present. I liked the colors becuase they were pastel. I thought I could base the rest of the webpages off of it. It looked both clean and colorful, elements that would be visually pleasing to the eye. I went with the idea of "simple is better" to not over complicate things, especially because I had a lot of trouble and found AI as a necessary friend that helped my find those colors and elements. I repeated the animations on each page to keep it consistent, and switched around colors. The pictures also came from the show.

Overall, my design prioritizes both color and creativity for user experience. I made due with my html/css knowledge limitations and became impressed by such a simple design. The combination of playful fonts, bright color gradients, and interactive elements is intended to create a friendly-fun experience that resembles aspects of the show Adventure Time for the user.
