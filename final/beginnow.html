Perspective drawing: creating depth using particular drawing techniques from different angles that are looking at the subjects. There are three main types of perspective.
Vanishing points: a point(s) where all perspective lines meet/cross at.
Perspective lines: lines created that go to the vanishing point.
Horizon line: a horizontal line representing the viewers eye level
