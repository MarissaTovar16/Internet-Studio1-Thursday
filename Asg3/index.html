How can framing your project in a story format help you refine your ideas?

  • Stories use the structure (beginning, middle, end) that can be used to create a captivating experience for users. If there is a strong story line, the audience can resonate-- they become invested in the product. The way we can get good user interaction is by looking at it as a story first. People subconsciously have a certain idea of what general good design is and how it looks. By framing my project as a story, it will help simplify my scattered ideas, keep user experience as a primary concern, and provide a clear project idea so that I can create an efficient website for users to enjoy without poor design.
