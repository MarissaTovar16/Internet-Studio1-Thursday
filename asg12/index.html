TOP 12 FAVORITE MOVIES

I look out for movies with exceptional animation as well as a fascinating plot. These movies have beautiful camera work, color grading, and animation. I really love when movies stand out to me and all 12 of these ones do just that.

Rango is an animated movie about a pet lizard who finds himself in the middle of the desert. He makes his way to a small town called Dirt where he faces the idea to become anyone he wanats to be.

Kill Bill, directed by Quentin Tarantino, is an awesome vengeful movie about a woman who kills the people who hurt her, with her main goal to kill bill.

The Grand Butapest Hotel tells a story about the adventures of a legendary concierge, Monsieur Gustave H., and his protégé, lobby boy Zero Moustafa.

Fantastic Mr.Fox is a story about a clever fox who steals from three mean farmers—Boggis, Bunce, and Bean—to feed his family.

Coraline is a dark stop motion animation about a girl who moves into a new house and discovers its weird mysteries.

Edward Scissorhands is a Tim Burton film where a man with scissors for hands enters the suburban life in 60s

The Conjuring is a horror movie based on true events, about a family who moves into a sinister house. They experience paranormal activity and eventually call for the help of proffesional paranormal investigators, Ed and Lorraine Warren.

Mid 90s tells the narrative of teen kids who skate.

Totoro is a Studio Ghibli movie that explores the adventure a yoing girl encounters between her and a magical creature called Totoro.

Donnie Darko is a psychological thriller about a story about a clever fox who steals from three mean farmers—Boggis, Bunce, and Bean—to feed his family.

Black Swan is about a ballerina’s descent into madness as she struggles to portray both the innocent White Swan and the sensual Black Swan in Swan Lake

Piece By Piece is an animated LEGO biography of artist Pharrell Williams.
